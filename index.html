About Me

Hi! I'm Actaeon, a son of Aristeus and Autonoe. I was brought up by the Centaur Chiron who taught me the art of hunting.

My prefferd hobby is hunting. One day I was devoured by my own dogs on Mount Cithaeron. The story goes that the goddess Artemis punished me for having been seen naked when she was bathing. She turned me into a stag and incited my own hounds to fury and set them on me.

They ate me without recognizing me and then hunted for me in vain throughout the forest. Finally, their search brought them to the cave where Chiron lived, and he made a statue of me to calm them.
